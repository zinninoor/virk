about us

VISIONING GROWTH, STEADY AND STRONG.

The early years were marked with modesty and our beginnings humble. For over a decade we had grown from strength to strength with a strong attitude towards the way we conduct our business. Our purpose is to provide quality poultry that are affordable with exceptional nutritional quality. Our products have already earned good will of the households. Our Vision is to “Provide best food at the best overall value with nutritional, quality and technological innovations in nominal cost”..

More

Our Vision

The way we conduct our business testifies the values we hold. We understand that growth is not just money and sales, rather bounteous in all aspects of business and life. The idea behind enriching lives is the whole perception of the business we do and we do it with a larger vision. Our vision was to build an integrated model that includes sourcing, feed production, hatcheries, breeding and contract farms under a single entity.

Our Product

Assuring quality, time after time
We are contented to bring goodness to people, in every bite. A lot goes in to every stage of growing the right chicken for you, quality meat that is always tender. Chicken is versatile. Roasted, boiled, grilled or a curry, combined with a wide range of spices and masalas, chicken makes a delicious, palatable and a nutritious meal. It is no wonder chicken is the world's primary source of animal protein and a healthy alternative to red meat.
